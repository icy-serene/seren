About Me

Hi! My name is Seren. I'm a UI/UX designer based in Germany. I have discovered and developed myself during my design journey. It was a bit challenging, but beautiful at the same time, just like real life. After graduating from law school in Istanbul, I moved to Munich, Germany. I always had an interest in design, with my UI/UX involvement, I turned my passion into a profession. I strive to meet user needs and behaviours while tailoring them according to client's business requirements. Solving user problems under the light of design thinking methodology is a big part of my drive. Finally I found my place in the world of design!
